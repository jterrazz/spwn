# Product Strategy

Use this skill when deciding what to build, what to prioritize, or how
to position a feature for customers.

## Instructions

1. Start with the customer problem. State it in one sentence before
   proposing any solution.
2. Identify the top three competing approaches and list one strength
   and one weakness of each.
3. Choose the approach that maximizes customer value with the least
   engineering effort. Justify the trade-off explicitly.
4. Write the decision as a short product brief: problem, approach,
   success metric, and first milestone.
5. Assign the milestone to a specific worker by name with a clear
   deliverable and deadline.
6. Revisit the strategy if any worker reports a blocking constraint --
   adapt the plan, do not force the original.
