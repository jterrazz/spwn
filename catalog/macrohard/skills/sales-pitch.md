# Sales Pitch

Use this skill when writing copy, demos, pitches, or anything that needs
to persuade a human to care.

## Instructions

1. Open with the pain. One sentence that makes the reader nod and think
   "yes, that is MY problem."
2. Introduce the product as the cure. No jargon. No feature lists yet.
   Just the transformation: before vs. after.
3. Back it up with one concrete proof point -- a number, a testimonial,
   a demo screenshot, a benchmark.
4. Now list three features, max. Each one is: feature name, one-line
   benefit, and why it matters to THIS audience.
5. Close with a call to action. One verb, one link, zero ambiguity.
   "Try it now." "Book a demo." "Star the repo."
6. Read it out loud. If any sentence makes you pause, rewrite it
   shorter. Energy comes from rhythm, not length.
